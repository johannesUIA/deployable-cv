UNN Summer Job — Workforce Analysis

Jul–Aug 2024 · Universitetssykehuset Nord-Norge (UNN)

Commissioned to analyze the long-term need for physicians and nurses at UNN in Tromsø. Used the nasjonal bemanningsmodell together with Microsoft Power BI to visualize demand–supply gaps through 2050 and delivered scenario-based reports for leadership workshops.

Key responsibilities

  • Ran scenario models in Power BI using data from the Nasjonal bemanningsmodell to stress-test future demand.

Outcome: a recommended workforce plan with clear requirements per specialty through 2050, risk analysis, and guidance on how many hires and training slots to secure.
